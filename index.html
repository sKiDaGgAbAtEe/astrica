🌙   Who is Alex?

A fairy who wandered out of the celestial realm and somehow ended up on Twitch hosting game shows for mortals. Alex lives under moonlight, games under starlight, and brings her whole heart to every stream. Expect chaos, unexpected kindness, and at least one person accidentally winning a tournament they weren't ready for.
